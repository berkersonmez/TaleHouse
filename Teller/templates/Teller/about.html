{% trans 'What is Tale House?' %}

{% trans 'Read interactive stories or write your own' %}

{% blocktrans %} Tale House is an online tool that lets you read fun interactive tales and stories. Everything in Tale House is about having fun with make-your-own stories. Select your path throughout the fables to have your unique experience. Better yet, you can make your dreams come true and write your own interactive stories with Tale House's easy-to-use interface. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Clean reading interface' %}

{% blocktrans %} Tale House presents stories in a clean and tidy way. Reading does not hurt your eyes. Compliance of background and foreground is designated according to provide the best experience to the readers. Reading in Tale House is just comfortable as reading a book from its paper print. Basic interface does not go in your way or interrupt your attention. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Visual Tale Representation' %}

{% blocktrans %} You can write your dream tale with the easy and innovative interface. Tale House aims to give a good representation of the tale links and paths. This makes it possible to design the tale on the go and come up with new ideas. The interface is designed to be light as well as comprehensive. With the aim of offering both simplicity and good usability, Tale House provides you with a great interactive story making tool. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Mobile Ready' %}

{% blocktrans %} Tale House is ready for mobile use, thanks to its responsive design. You can read stories on the go using your smart phone or tablet. All functionality of the tool is open to access with mobile devices. iOS, Android, Windows Phone and Blackberry are all supported. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Tale House is open source' %}

{% blocktrans with link='Github' %} Tale House is open source, which means you can see its codes and also contribute to it! It is dedicated to the community, thus, is completely free. It is created using the great Django framework. You can see the repository at {{ link }} and make contributions to carry it forward to be a perfect tool. {% endblocktrans %}

How to use Tale House

{% trans 'Create a tale' %}

{% blocktrans %} Creating a tale is as easy as giving it a name. You can also designate a language in which you will write the tale. Readers will be able to filter the tales that are in their languages to select and read them. Also, tales can be designated as 'poll' tales; which means the tale is not designated by individual readers uniquely, but it is developed by community votes. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Many a little makes a mickle' %}

{% blocktrans %} Tales consist of tale parts. Think of them as book chapters, or maybe something smaller. Develop the story in the tale parts to carry the readers to another choice that they will make. Punish bad choices, or create plot twists and cliffhangers; it is all up to your imagination! If you are creating a poll tale, don't forget to give the part a 'poll end date' which will show the time the poll is going to end and the story is going to progress. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Let users make choices' %}

{% blocktrans %} Tale links bind parts together. They are the choices that readers will make. Let them choose between giving money away vs. saving it; going through dark corridors vs. facing the known enemy; sacrificing wealth vs. health; sparing a life vs. killing a bug... Divaricate the story as much as you want and give the readers a thrilling experience. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans 'Explore' %}

{% blocktrans %} You can also read others' stories, follow users and see their tales, search the tale for your liking, give rating to the polls you read to guide other users. Just choose your path to start your brave adventure! {% endblocktrans %}
